About this game

This is a real world game originally developed by

We loved the concept of Super Fight, but when out and about never had a deck handy. The logical thing was to look for an online generator, but either due to a lack of searching abilities or lack of availablity, we couldn't find one. The next logical step was then to make one ourselves, however, there also seemed to be a decided lack in experience (and time) when it came to developing such an app, thus the OpenFight app was born as an educational project.

It's open source, so enjoy! And show your support by contributing.
